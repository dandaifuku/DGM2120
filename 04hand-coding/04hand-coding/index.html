Gojira

Gojira is a metal band with four band members, Joe Duplantier, Mario Duplantier, Christian Andreu, and Jean-Micheal Labadie. With Joe singing, Joe and Christian on guitar, Mario on Drums, and Jean-Micheal on bass, they compose progressive/heavy metal.

Band Members

Joe Duplantier
Plays guitar and sings, he is a co-founder with his brother, Mario!
Mario Duplantier
Plays drums and won best metal drummer of the year, 2021!
Christian Andreu
Plays guitar and has beautiful, silky black hair he swings around!
Jean-Micheal Labadie
Plays bass and is probably the most energetic one on stage!
